A biochemical estimate of biological aging based on recent lab panels.

info

Reference Context

This estimate utilizes the Levine PhenoAge model, validated primarily against the NHANES (US) cohort. Because clinical baselines vary across ethnicities, this value is most effective as an internal trend marker rather than an absolute diagnostic threshold for the Indian demographic.

Source: Aging (Albany NY), 2018
